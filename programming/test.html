    About Us

    Safe Space was created for people that are looking for a break from life. Sometimes living is exhausting so we design a website in which you can find places to recharge your batteries, find inspiration and breath the feeling of being alive. We offer different services, like maps with hidden places where you can escape reality for how long you want, test to know yourself better, have the advice of experts and the possibility to know other people that might be in the same situation as you! We created a place where you can be yourself, a nest to hide and leave the darkness behind.
